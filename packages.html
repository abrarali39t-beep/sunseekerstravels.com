Sample Itinerary

5-Day Complete Kashmir — Day by Day

A glimpse into how we plan your journey. Every trip is customized to your preferences and pace.

D1
Arrival in Srinagar — Welcome to Paradise
+

Your Kashmir journey begins the moment you land. We'll be at the airport with a name board to escort you to your vehicle for a warm, hassle-free start.

🛬Airport Pickup — Sheikh ul Alam International Airport transfer to hotel
🌊Dal Lake Shikara Ride — An evening on the iconic lake watching the sun set over the mountains
🌸Mughal Garden Drive — Nishat Bagh & Shalimar Bagh at golden hour
🏨Hotel / Houseboat Check-in — Rest and prepare for Day 2
D2
Gulmarg — The Meadow of Flowers
+

A full day in one of Asia's top ski destinations. Whether you're here for snow sports or just the jaw-dropping scenery, Gulmarg delivers.

🚡Gondola Ride — Phase 1 (2,690m) & Phase 2 (3,979m) for panoramic Himalayan views
⛷️Snow Activities — Skiing, snowboarding, sledging (seasonal & optional)
🏔️Apharwat Peak — The stunning summit view in winter
🍵Kehwa Break — Traditional Kashmiri tea with kangri (fire pot)
D3
Pahalgam — Valley of Shepherds
+

Pahalgam's lush green meadows and crystal-clear Lidder river make it one of Kashmir's most beloved destinations.

🌿Betaab Valley — Named after the Bollywood classic; stunning alpine meadow
🏞️Aru Valley — Pristine meadow and ideal trekking base
🐴Pony Rides — Optional horse rides through the valley (not included)
🌊Lidder River Walk — Evening stroll along the glacial river
D4
Sonmarg — The Golden Meadow
+

The drive to Sonmarg is as spectacular as the destination itself. Winding roads, glacial rivers, and Himalayan peaks all the way.

🏔️Thajiwas Glacier — Pony or walk to the famous glacier
🌄Zoji La Pass Viewpoint — One of India's most scenic mountain passes
🐟Sindh River — Famous for trout fishing (optional)
🌅Sunset Drive Back — Golden hour return to Srinagar
D5
Doodhpathri & Departure
+

End your Kashmir journey on a peaceful high note — the "Bowl of Milk" meadow that most tourists never discover.

🌾Doodhpathri — Pristine alpine meadow with milky white streams from melting snow
🛍️Srinagar Shopping — Pashmina, saffron, walnut wood crafts, and Kashmiri carpets
🛫Airport Drop — Comfortable, timely transfer for your flight home
