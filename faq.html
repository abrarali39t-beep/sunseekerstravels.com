🚗 Vehicles & Fleet

Which vehicle should I choose for my Kashmir trip? +
Here's a quick guide based on group size and comfort:
  • 1–4 people: Swift Dzire or Honda Amaze (sedans) — economical and comfortable
  • 4–7 people: Toyota Innova or Innova Crysta — the most popular Kashmir choice

Yes — all our vehicles are fully air-conditioned. All vehicles are also mountain-road certified and regularly serviced specifically for Kashmir's terrain, including:

Do your vehicles have luggage space for large groups? +

🏔️ Tours & Destinations

What is the best time to visit Kashmir? +
Kashmir is beautiful year-round, but each season offers something different:
  • Spring (March–May): Tulip gardens, cherry blossoms, mild weather — most popular
  • Summer (June–August): Perfect temperature (15–25°C), all roads open, ideal for trekking
  • Autumn (Sept–Nov): Golden chinar leaves, crisp air, less crowded — photographers' favourite
  • Winter (Dec–Feb): Snowfall, skiing in Gulmarg, frozen Dal Lake — magical but cold
There is truly no "bad time" — just a different type of Kashmir magic in every season.
Is it safe to travel to Kashmir? Is the road to Gulmarg / Sonmarg safe in winter? +
Yes — Kashmir is very safe for tourists. The valley welcomes millions of visitors every year and tourism is thriving. Our drivers are experienced locals who:
  • Know every road condition and seasonal route
  • Monitor weather and road closure alerts daily
  • Have emergency contacts and safe stopover points mapped
  • Never compromise on passenger safety for speed
In winter, mountain roads can be temporarily closed after heavy snowfall. We will always inform you in advance and suggest alternatives or rescheduling.
Can you arrange hotel or houseboat accommodation? +
While accommodation is not included in our standard vehicle packages, we have strong partnerships with hotels, guest houses, and houseboat owners across Kashmir. We can recommend and coordinate bookings for:
  • Traditional Kashmiri houseboats on Dal Lake
  • Boutique hotels in Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam
  • Budget guesthouses for backpackers
  • Luxury resorts for premium travellers
Just let us know your preference and budget when booking, and we'll set everything up.
Can you cover Ladakh (Leh) from Srinagar? +
Yes! The Srinagar–Leh Highway is one of the most spectacular drives in the world. We offer Leh–Ladakh transfers via:
  • Srinagar → Leh via Sonamarg–Zoji La (open June–October)
  • Multi-day Ladakh exploration packages
  • Leh airport pickup and Nubra/Pangong tours
Contact us for availability and pricing — Leh routes require advance planning and special vehicle arrangements.

ℹ️ General Questions

Do I need any special permit to travel in Kashmir as an Indian tourist? +
No. Indian citizens can travel freely to all tourist areas of Jammu & Kashmir including Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, and Sonmarg without any special permit. Only certain restricted border areas (like parts near the Line of Control) require additional permissions, and those are not typical tourist destinations. Foreign nationals may require a Protected Area Permit (PAP) for certain areas — we can advise on this based on your nationality.
What should I pack for a Kashmir trip? +
Packing depends on the season, but here are universal essentials:
  • Year-round: Comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen (high altitude = strong UV), light jacket for evenings
  • Winter: Heavy thermal layers, waterproof jacket, snow boots, gloves, woolen socks
  • Summer: Light cotton clothes, rain poncho (July–August), sunglasses
  • All seasons: Power bank (limited charging at scenic spots), camera, valid ID
We're happy to give personalized packing advice based on your specific itinerary — just ask!
