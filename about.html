Our Story

A Journey That Began With a Dream

Sun Seekers Travels was founded with a simple yet powerful vision — to make exploring Kashmir accessible, safe, and unforgettable for every traveller who sets foot in this paradise on Earth.

Based in Zia Complex, Soura, Srinagar, we have been the trusted travel companion for hundreds of tourists, families, corporate groups, and honeymooners from across India and abroad.

Our deep roots in Kashmir give us an unmatched advantage — we know every hidden gem, every safe mountain road, and every perfect sunrise viewpoint that makes this valley legendary.
